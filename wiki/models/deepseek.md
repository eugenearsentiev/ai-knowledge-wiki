# DeepSeek

A Chinese AI lab (founded 2023 as a research unit of High-Flyer, a quantitative hedge fund) that released a series of high-performing open-weight models. Notable for achieving performance competitive with frontier models at significantly lower training cost.

**Key models** — DeepSeek-V2 (efficient Mixture-of-Experts architecture), DeepSeek-V3, DeepSeek-R1 (reasoning model trained largely via [[reinforcement-learning|RL]] without extensive supervised fine-tuning, January 2025).

**Why it matters** — DeepSeek-R1 demonstrated that efficient RL training can match [[openai|OpenAI]]'s o1 on reasoning benchmarks with far less compute. Sparked significant discussion about compute efficiency and scaling law assumptions.

**Open-weight** — DeepSeek releases model weights publicly, making it a leading open-weight alternative alongside [[llama|Llama]] and [[mistral|Mistral]].
