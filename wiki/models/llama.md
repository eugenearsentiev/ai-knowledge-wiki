# Llama

The open-weight model family from [[meta-ai|Meta AI]]. Released starting February 2023 (Llama 1), with Llama 2 (July 2023), Llama 3 (2024), and Llama 4 (2025). The dominant open-weight LLM series; the community has built thousands of fine-tuned variants on top of it.

**Why it matters** — releasing strong model weights publicly democratized LLM experimentation. Researchers and developers can run, fine-tune, and study the models directly rather than depending on API access.

**Open-weight vs. open-source** — the weights are released, but under Meta's acceptable-use license (not fully permissive). Downloadable but not Apache-2.0.

**Local deployment** — runnable via [[ollama|Ollama]], llama.cpp, and [[lm-studio|LM Studio]] with [[quantization|quantization]] to fit consumer hardware.

**Community ecosystem** — [[hugging-face|Hugging Face]] hosts the weights and hundreds of fine-tuned variants; [[lora|LoRA]]-based fine-tuning is the standard adaptation method.
