# Gemini

[[google-deepmind|Google DeepMind]]'s flagship model family, launched December 2023. Designed as natively multimodal — trained on text, images, audio, and video together rather than adding modality support post-hoc.

**Tiers** — Ultra (most capable), Pro (balanced), Nano (on-device). Successor to PaLM and the Bard product.

**Distribution** — powers Google Search AI overviews, Google Workspace features, and the Gemini.google.com interface. Available via API through Google AI Studio and Vertex AI.

**Context window** — Gemini 1.5 Pro introduced a 1M-token context window, enabling document-scale reasoning over very long inputs.

**Competition** — competes directly with [[gpt|GPT-4]] / o-series ([[openai|OpenAI]]) and [[claude|Claude]] ([[anthropic|Anthropic]]) at the frontier.
