# GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer)

[[openai|OpenAI]]'s flagship model family. A series of decoder-only [[transformer|Transformer]] models trained on broad text corpora, growing from GPT (2018) through GPT-2, GPT-3, GPT-4, and the o-series reasoning models.

**Key milestones** — GPT-3 (2020) showed that scale alone produced surprising emergent capabilities. InstructGPT added [[rlhf|RLHF]] to produce helpful behavior. GPT-4 added vision input and substantially stronger reasoning.

**o-series (2024–2025)** — o1, o3 use extended chain-of-thought at inference time for harder reasoning tasks; a different scaling axis than simply larger base models.

**Model vs. product** — GPT-4 is the model; [[chatgpt|ChatGPT]] is the consumer product built on top.

**Impact** — GPT-3 kickstarted the current LLM wave; [[chatgpt|ChatGPT]] (GPT-3.5-based) was the public inflection point for [[generative-ai|generative AI]].
