# AI Ecosystem Overview

The field nests inside itself. **[[machine-learning|Machine Learning]]** (learning from data) sits inside AI; **[[deep-learning|Deep Learning]]** (many-layered neural networks) sits inside ML; and **[[generative-ai|Generative AI]]** — including **[[llm|LLMs]]** — is the most visible slice of deep learning today. Every LLM is deep learning, every deep learning model is ML, and all of it is AI — but the reverse isn't true.

Cutting across that nesting are application-oriented **subfields**: [[nlp|NLP]], [[computer-vision|Computer Vision]], [[reinforcement-learning|Reinforcement Learning]], [[speech|Speech]], [[robotics|Robotics]], [[recommender-systems|Recommender Systems]], and [[multimodal-ai|Multimodal AI]]. These are the top-level map; most tools and models belong to one or more of them.

The current landscape is shaped by a handful of organizations: [[openai|OpenAI]], [[anthropic|Anthropic]], [[google-deepmind|Google DeepMind]], [[meta-ai|Meta AI]], [[mistral|Mistral]], [[deepseek|DeepSeek]], and [[hugging-face|Hugging Face]].

The [[timeline|history]] runs from symbolic AI in the 1950s through two AI winters, the statistical turn, and the [[transformer|Transformer]] era. The throughline: general methods that scale with computation tend to win — see [[bitter-lesson|The Bitter Lesson]].
