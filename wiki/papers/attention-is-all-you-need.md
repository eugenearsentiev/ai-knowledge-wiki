# "Attention Is All You Need" (2017)

The paper that introduced the **[[transformer|Transformer]]** architecture. Published at NeurIPS 2017 by Google researchers: Vaswani, Shazeer, Parmar, Uszkoreit, Jones, Gomez, Kaiser, Polosukhin.

**Core contribution** — a sequence model built entirely on attention mechanisms, with no recurrence or convolution, that outperformed [[rnn-lstm|RNNs and LSTMs]] on machine translation while training much faster due to full parallelism.

**Impact** — arguably the most consequential ML paper since [[alexnet-imagenet|AlexNet]]. The Transformer became the foundation for [[bert|BERT]], [[gpt|GPT]], [[claude|Claude]], [[gemini|Gemini]], and virtually every modern large model. It eventually spread to vision (ViT), audio, and [[multimodal-ai|multimodal]] systems — not just language.

**Key authors** — Noam Shazeer (co-author; later at Character.AI). The 8 authors have collectively gone on to found or join most major AI labs.
