# ChatGPT

The consumer chat product from [[openai|OpenAI]], launched November 2022. Built on [[gpt|GPT]] models (initially GPT-3.5-turbo, then GPT-4 and beyond). Reached 100 million users in two months — the fastest consumer product adoption in history at the time of launch.

**Model vs. product** — ChatGPT is a product, not a model. GPT-4 is the model; ChatGPT is the interface (web app, mobile apps) with conversation history, image generation via [[dalle|DALL·E]], code interpreter, and web browsing.

**Impact** — the public inflection point for [[generative-ai|generative AI]]. Before ChatGPT, LLMs were known mainly to researchers and developers; afterward they became mainstream consumer technology.

**Tiers** — Free (GPT-4o limited), Plus (full GPT-4o access), Team, Enterprise (privacy and admin controls), Edu.
