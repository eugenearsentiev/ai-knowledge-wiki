# Generative AI

AI systems that produce new content — text, images, audio, code, video — rather than just classifying or predicting.

**Core technologies** — [[llm|Large Language Models]] for text and code; [[diffusion-models|diffusion models]] for images; related approaches for audio, music, and video. Many frontier systems are [[multimodal-ai|multimodal]], combining modalities in one model.

**Key model families** — [[gpt|GPT]] and [[claude|Claude]] (text/multimodal), [[gemini|Gemini]] (multimodal), [[stable-diffusion|Stable Diffusion]] and [[dalle|DALL·E]] (images).

**Key techniques** — [[prompting|Prompting]], [[fine-tuning|fine-tuning]], [[rlhf|RLHF]], [[lora|LoRA]], [[rag|RAG]].

**Position** — subset of [[deep-learning|Deep Learning]]; the most visible AI application category since late 2022. The public inflection point was [[chatgpt|ChatGPT]] (November 2022).

**Key organizations** — [[openai|OpenAI]], [[anthropic|Anthropic]], [[google-deepmind|Google DeepMind]], [[meta-ai|Meta AI]], [[mistral|Mistral]], [[deepseek|DeepSeek]].
