# Computer Vision

The subfield of AI that interprets images and video. Tasks range from image classification (what is this?) to object detection (where are things?) to segmentation (pixel-level labeling), generation, and video understanding.

**Key methods** — [[cnn|Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)]] dominated from [[alexnet-imagenet|AlexNet (2012)]] through the 2010s. The [[transformer|Transformer]] (Vision Transformer / ViT) has increasingly taken over state-of-the-art vision tasks. [[diffusion-models|Diffusion models]] power the leading image generators.

**Notable models** — [[stable-diffusion|Stable Diffusion]], [[dalle|DALL·E]], Midjourney (text-to-image); CLIP bridges vision and language.

**Links to other subfields** — [[multimodal-ai|Multimodal AI]] combines vision with text and audio; [[robotics|Robotics]] depends on vision for perception.

**Key contributors** — [[yann-lecun|Yann LeCun]] (CNNs), [[geoffrey-hinton|Geoffrey Hinton]] (AlexNet lineage), [[ilya-sutskever|Ilya Sutskever]] (AlexNet).
