# Recommender Systems

AI systems that predict what a user will want next — products to buy, content to watch, songs to play, ads to show. Among the highest-economic-impact applications of ML, deployed by Netflix, YouTube, Spotify, Amazon, and TikTok.

**Core approaches** — collaborative filtering (learn from user-item interaction patterns), content-based filtering (match item features to user preferences), and hybrid systems. Deep learning models dominate large-scale deployments.

**Key methods** — matrix factorization, neural collaborative filtering, two-tower models (separate encoders for users and items, dot-product similarity at query time), session-based models using [[transformer|Transformers]].

**Adjacent areas** — ranking and retrieval (closely related in search), [[nlp|NLP]] for understanding item content, [[reinforcement-learning|RL]] for optimizing long-term engagement rather than immediate clicks.

**Position** — a subfield of [[machine-learning|ML]] with its own specialized problem framing; mostly distinct from the LLM wave, though LLMs are beginning to be applied here.
