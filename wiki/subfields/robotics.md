# Robotics

The field combining AI with physical systems — perception, planning, and control of robots operating in the real world. AI's role is primarily in perception (understanding sensor data) and policy learning (deciding what actions to take).

**AI methods applied** — [[computer-vision|Computer Vision]] for perception; [[reinforcement-learning|RL]] for learning control policies; [[transformer|Transformer]]-based models increasingly used as unified policies; [[multimodal-ai|Multimodal AI]] for language-conditioned robot control.

**Key challenges** — sim-to-real transfer (training in simulation, deploying physically), data scarcity, safety, real-time constraints.

**Trend** — "foundation models for robotics": using large pretrained models (especially language-vision models) as general-purpose backbones for robot control, rather than hand-engineering each skill.

**Links** — [[reinforcement-learning|RL]], [[computer-vision|Computer Vision]], [[multimodal-ai|Multimodal AI]], [[google-deepmind|Google DeepMind]] (robotics research).
