# Speech AI

The subfield handling the audio-language boundary. **Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR)** converts spoken audio to text; **Text-to-Speech (TTS)** synthesizes speech from text. Also covers speaker identification, voice activity detection, and speech enhancement.

**Key methods** — early statistical models (Hidden Markov Models) gave way to deep learning. The [[transformer|Transformer]] now dominates both directions. Whisper ([[openai|OpenAI]]) is the most widely used open ASR model; modern TTS systems achieve near-human quality.

**Connections** — Speech is the audio edge of [[nlp|NLP]] and the human-interface layer of [[multimodal-ai|Multimodal AI]]. Frontier multimodal models ([[gemini|Gemini]], [[claude|Claude]]) handle text, images, and audio in a unified system.

**Practical relevance** — voice assistants, real-time transcription, accessibility tools, and conversational AI all depend on this subfield.
