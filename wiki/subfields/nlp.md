# Natural Language Processing (NLP)

The subfield of AI concerned with understanding and generating human language. NLP is the home turf of [[llm|Large Language Models]].

**Core tasks** — text classification, named entity recognition, machine translation, question answering, summarization, sentiment analysis, text generation.

**Key methods** — the field progressed from rule-based and statistical approaches (bag-of-words, TF-IDF, n-gram models) through [[rnn-lstm|RNNs and LSTMs]] to the [[transformer|Transformer]], which now dominates. Pre-trained models like [[bert|BERT]] (encoding) and the [[gpt|GPT]] family (generation) are the standard starting points.

**Subfield boundaries** — NLP is the text layer; [[speech|Speech]] handles audio-to-text and text-to-audio at the edges; [[multimodal-ai|Multimodal AI]] extends across modalities.

**Key orgs** — [[openai|OpenAI]], [[anthropic|Anthropic]], [[google-deepmind|Google DeepMind]], [[meta-ai|Meta AI]], [[hugging-face|Hugging Face]] (library ecosystem).

**Deep-dive candidates** — tokenization, embeddings, prompt engineering, retrieval → flag for focused KB.
