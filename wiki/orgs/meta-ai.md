# Meta AI

The AI research and product division of Meta (formerly Facebook). Creator of the [[llama|Llama]] model family and a significant open-source contributor to the field.

**Open-weight strategy** — Meta releases Llama weights publicly, taking a more open stance than [[openai|OpenAI]] or [[anthropic|Anthropic]]. Rationale: openness as competitive moat (the ecosystem builds on Llama), regulatory positioning, and democratizing access.

**Research contributions** — [[llama|Llama]], PyTorch (the dominant deep learning framework), FAISS (widely used vector search library), and extensive [[nlp|NLP]] and [[computer-vision|vision]] research from FAIR (Fundamental AI Research).

**Key figures** — [[yann-lecun|Yann LeCun]] (Chief AI Scientist; skeptical of current LLM path to AGI; advocates for "world model" architectures).

**Products** — Meta AI assistant embedded in WhatsApp, Instagram, and Facebook; Llama API.
