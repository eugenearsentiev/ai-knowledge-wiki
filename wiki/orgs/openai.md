# OpenAI

San Francisco AI lab founded in 2015, initially as a nonprofit. Creator of the [[gpt|GPT]] model family, [[chatgpt|ChatGPT]], [[dalle|DALL·E]], Whisper (ASR), and Codex (code). The most influential organization in the current AI wave.

**Key products** — [[chatgpt|ChatGPT]] (consumer), API (developer), [[gpt|GPT-4 / o-series]] (models), [[dalle|DALL·E]] (image generation), Whisper (speech recognition).

**Structure** — converted to a capped-profit structure; complex relationship with its nonprofit parent board. Microsoft has made multi-billion-dollar investments and integrates OpenAI models into Azure and Copilot.

**Founding figures** — Sam Altman (CEO), [[ilya-sutskever|Ilya Sutskever]] (co-founder, departed 2024), Elon Musk (early backer, departed).

**Research legacy** — GPT-3, InstructGPT, [[rlhf|RLHF]] at scale, [[dalle|DALL·E]], scaling laws papers, o1 reasoning.

**Links** — [[gpt|GPT]], [[chatgpt|ChatGPT]], [[dalle|DALL·E]], [[rlhf|RLHF]], [[ilya-sutskever|Sutskever]].
