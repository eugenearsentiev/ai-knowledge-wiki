# Anthropic

AI safety company founded in 2021 by former [[openai|OpenAI]] researchers, including Dario Amodei (CEO) and Daniela Amodei (President). Creator of the [[claude|Claude]] model family.

**Mission** — safety-focused AI development alongside commercial model building. Conducts interpretability (mechanistic analysis of transformer internals) and alignment research. Constitutional AI is its flagship alignment technique — a variant of [[rlhf|RLHF]] guided by a written set of principles.

**Scale** — Amazon is a major investor and cloud partner (AWS). Valued at ~$61B as of late 2024.

**Products** — [[claude|Claude]] models (API + Claude.ai interface + Claude Code CLI).

**Research contributions** — Constitutional AI, mechanistic interpretability (neuron and circuit-level analysis of transformer behavior), scaling-law contributions, Claude's long-context capabilities.

**Links** — [[claude|Claude]], [[rlhf|RLHF]], [[llm|LLM]], [[openai|OpenAI]] (founding lineage).
