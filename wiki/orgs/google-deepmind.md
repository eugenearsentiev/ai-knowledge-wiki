# Google DeepMind

The AI research division of Alphabet/Google, formed in 2023 by merging Google Brain and DeepMind (originally acquired from the UK in 2014). Creator of [[gemini|Gemini]], AlphaGo, AlphaFold, and more.

**Research legacy** — AlphaGo (game playing, landmark [[reinforcement-learning|RL]] result), AlphaFold (protein structure prediction), AlphaCode (code generation), [[attention-is-all-you-need|"Attention Is All You Need"]] (from Google Brain), [[bert|BERT]], PaLM, Gemini.

**Products** — [[gemini|Gemini]] models via Gemini.google.com and Google AI Studio; integration into Google Search, Workspace, and Android.

**DeepMind side** — historically focused on fundamental research and scientific applications (biology, mathematics, robotics). The merger unified research capacity.

**Key figures** — Demis Hassabis (CEO, DeepMind side), Noam Shazeer (Transformer co-author), Jeff Dean.
