# Mistral AI

French AI startup founded in 2023 by former [[google-deepmind|Google DeepMind]] and [[meta-ai|Meta AI]] researchers. Known for releasing efficient, high-performing open-weight models.

**Key models** — Mistral 7B (2023, outperformed larger models at its size), Mixtral 8x7B (Mixture-of-Experts architecture — activates only a subset of parameters per token for efficiency), Mistral Large / Le Chat (frontier-class), Codestral (code).

**Open-weight commitment** — several models released under Apache 2.0 (fully permissive), making them notable for commercial use without licensing restrictions. More permissive than [[llama|Llama]]'s usage policy.

**Architecture highlight** — Mixtral's Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) design showed that sparse activation can match dense models of much larger size, influencing later architectures including [[deepseek|DeepSeek]] and [[gemini|Gemini]].
