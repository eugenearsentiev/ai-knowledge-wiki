# Andrej Karpathy

Slovak-Canadian researcher; one of the most influential practitioners and educators in modern AI.

**Career** — co-founder at [[openai|OpenAI]], Director of AI at Tesla (led Autopilot neural network approach), returned to [[openai|OpenAI]] (2023), departed (2024) to work independently on AI education and research.

**Key contributions** — ImageNet-winning CNN work during PhD at Stanford ([[computer-vision|CV]]), Tesla Autopilot's camera-only neural network philosophy, influential blog posts and tutorials.

**Educational impact** — YouTube series "Neural Networks: Zero to Hero" (micrograd → makemore → nanoGPT) is among the most widely used self-study resources for learning [[deep-learning|deep learning]] from first principles. [karpathy.github.io](https://karpathy.github.io).

**This wiki** — the llm-wiki concept that inspired this KB was first proposed by Karpathy as an idea file for building a compiled personal knowledge base.
