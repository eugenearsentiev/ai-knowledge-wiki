# Yoshua Bengio

Canadian computer scientist; full professor at Université de Montréal, founder and scientific director of Mila (Quebec AI Institute). One of the three "Godfathers of Deep Learning."

**Key contributions** — foundational work on neural language models (predating the Transformer era), early attention mechanism work (with Bahdanau, 2014 — a key precursor to [[attention-is-all-you-need|Attention Is All You Need]]), deep generative models, and representation learning theory.

**Recognition** — 2018 Turing Award (shared with [[geoffrey-hinton|Hinton]] and [[yann-lecun|LeCun]]).

**Current focus** — strong AI safety advocate; signed major open letters on AI risks. Works on AI safety research alongside technical ML contributions. Concerned about the societal and existential implications of frontier models.
