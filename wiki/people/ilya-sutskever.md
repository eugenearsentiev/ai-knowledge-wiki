# Ilya Sutskever

Israeli-Canadian computer scientist. Co-founded [[openai|OpenAI]] (2015) and served as Chief Scientist until departing in May 2024. One of the most technically influential figures in the current AI wave.

**Key contributions** — co-authored the [[alexnet-imagenet|AlexNet]] paper with Krizhevsky and [[geoffrey-hinton|Hinton]] (2012, as Hinton's PhD student), co-founded OpenAI, led the technical work on GPT-3, InstructGPT, and the systems behind [[chatgpt|ChatGPT]].

**Post-OpenAI** — founded Safe Superintelligence (SSI) in 2024, focused purely on building safe superintelligence without the pressure of shipping consumer products.

**Role in AlexNet** — as [[geoffrey-hinton|Hinton]]'s PhD student at Toronto, Sutskever co-designed and co-trained AlexNet — the result that launched the deep learning era. A direct through-line from that 2012 paper to the GPT era.
