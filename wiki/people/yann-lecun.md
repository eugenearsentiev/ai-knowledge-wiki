# Yann LeCun

French-American computer scientist; VP and Chief AI Scientist at [[meta-ai|Meta AI]], professor at NYU. One of the three "Godfathers of Deep Learning."

**Key contributions** — pioneered [[cnn|Convolutional Neural Networks]] (LeNet, 1990s; applied to digit recognition and check processing), foundational backpropagation work with [[geoffrey-hinton|Hinton]], and strong advocacy for self-supervised learning as a path to general intelligence.

**Recognition** — 2018 Turing Award (shared with [[geoffrey-hinton|Hinton]] and [[yoshua-bengio|Bengio]]).

**Current views** — publicly skeptical of the current [[llm|LLM]]-centric path to AGI. Advocates for "world model" architectures that build internal representations of how the world works, not just text prediction. Debates with AI safety researchers about risk timelines.
